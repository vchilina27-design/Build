## Getting Started

### Installation & Launch

1. **Download** Tank Battle 3D from your platform (Steam, Epic Games, etc.)
2. **Install** the game to your preferred location
3. **Launch** the game and complete the initial setup
4. **Create an Account** (if required) or log in
5. **Download** required game assets (usually done automatically)

### First Time Setup

- **Graphics Settings**: The game auto-detects your system specs. Adjust if needed in Settings
- **Audio Setup**: Test your speakers/headphones in the Audio settings
- **Key Bindings**: Review and customize controls to your preference
- **Difficulty Level**: Start with "Easy" or "Normal" if you're new to tank games

### Game Overview

Tank Battle 3D is a fast-paced, tactical 3D combat game where you command a tank in intense multiplayer and single-player battles. Destroy enemy tanks, capture objectives, and lead your team to victory!

## Game Modes

### Campaign (Single-Player)

A story-driven experience with progressive difficulty:
- **Tutorial Missions**: Learn the basics
- **Story Campaign**: 20+ missions with increasing complexity
- **Challenges**: Special bonus missions with unique objectives

### Multiplayer Modes

#### Team Deathmatch (TDM)
- **Players**: 8v8
- **Objective**: Eliminate enemy tanks
- **Win Condition**: First team to 50 kills or highest score at time limit

#### Capture the Flag (CTF)
- **Players**: 6v6
- **Objective**: Capture enemy flag and bring it to your base
- **Strategy**: Teamwork is essential

#### Domination
- **Players**: 8v8
- **Objective**: Control 3 control points on the map
- **Scoring**: 1 point per second per controlled point

#### Survival (Wave-Based)
- **Players**: 1-4
- **Objective**: Defend your base against increasingly difficult waves of enemies
- **Rewards**: High loot and experience gains

### Custom Matches

Create private matches with custom rules, maps, and player counts.

## Tutorials

### Tutorial 1: Movement & Aiming

**Duration**: 5 minutes

1. Enter the **Practice Range** from the main menu
2. Practice moving your tank using WASD keys
3. Learn to separate tank movement from turret rotation
4. **Pro Tip**: Don't move directly at enemies; use strafing!

### Tutorial 2: Weapon Systems

**Duration**: 10 minutes

1. Familiarize yourself with the default cannon
2. Learn ammo types:
   - **Standard Rounds**: Balanced damage and ammo capacity
   - **Armor-Piercing Rounds**: High damage, lower ammo
   - **Explosive Rounds**: Area damage, good for crowds
3. Practice switching weapons with number keys
4. Understand reload times and magazine capacity

### Tutorial 3: Tank Classes

**Duration**: 8 minutes

Five tank classes available:

- **Light Tank**: Fast, low armor, high firepower. Best for scouts and hit-and-run attacks
- **Medium Tank**: Balanced stats across the board
- **Heavy Tank**: Slow, high armor, powerful cannon. Good for pushing lines
- **Tank Destroyer**: Very high damage, low armor. Sniper-style gameplay
- **Artillery Tank**: Long-range support, splash damage

Choose your playstyle and master your tank class!

### Tutorial 4: Map Navigation

**Duration**: 7 minutes

1. Press `M` to open the map
2. Learn spawn points and control points
3. Identify armor and ammo refill stations
4. Scout advantageous positions
5. Understand sightlines and cover locations

### Tutorial 5: Advanced Tactics

**Duration**: 15 minutes

- Master corner peeking and shoulder peeking
- Learn to angle your armor for maximum protection
- Use terrain to your advantage
- Coordinate with teammates
- Manage ammunition and health effectively

## Tips & Tricks

### Combat Tips

1. **Use Cover Wisely**
   - Always fight from behind cover
   - Peek from different angles to avoid predictability
   - Use terrain elevation to your advantage

2. **Master Tank Angling**
   - Angle your armor to increase effective protection
   - Show your thickest armor to enemies
   - Practice in the range before using in matches

3. **Ammunition Management**
   - Know your ammo capacity
   - Switch ammo types based on enemy tank class
   - Use armor-piercing rounds against heavily armored targets

4. **Positioning**
   - Stay with your team but don't bunch up
   - Play to your tank's strengths (speed, armor, firepower)
   - Control high ground when possible

5. **Spotting Enemies**
   - Report enemy positions to your team
   - Use the spotting system (hold `Alt` + click)
   - Keep eyes on the mini-map for teammate pings

### Economy Tips

- **Earn Credits**: Win matches, complete challenges, destroy enemies
- **Spend Wisely**: Upgrade tank armor before weapons
- **Premium Currency**: Use Gold for battle passes and cosmetics (not pay-to-win)
- **Daily Rewards**: Log in daily for free credits and loot

### Progression Tips

1. **Start with Campaign**: Teaches all fundamentals
2. **Play Practice Range**: Refine your aim and tank control
3. **Join Beginner Lobbies**: Find players at your skill level
4. **Watch Replays**: Study how experienced players play
5. **Master One Tank First**: Specialize before diversifying

## Strategies

### Beginner Strategies

#### Rush Strategy
- Quickly move to a favorable position early game
- Establish control point dominance
- Maintain your position with support from teammates

**Best For**: Light and Medium tanks

#### Defensive Strategy
- Control high-ground positions
- Let enemies come to you
- Focus on armor and survivability

**Best For**: Heavy tanks and Tank Destroyers

### Intermediate Strategies

#### Flanking Maneuver
1. Identify enemy positions
2. Move around enemy lines (use terrain)
3. Attack from the side where armor is weakest
4. Retreat if overwhelmed

**Timing**: Execute when teammates engage frontally

#### Support Role
- Stay near heavy tank allies
- Focus fire on targets they're attacking
- Protect their flanks and rear
- Fall back when needed

#### Hit & Run (Skirmish)
- Quick attacks on isolated enemies
- Never stay in one position long
- Retreat and reposition constantly
- Excellent for light tanks

### Advanced Strategies

#### Armor Degradation Tactics
- Concentrate fire on one tank to break their armor
- Spread focus when armor is weakened
- Finish damaged vehicles quickly

#### Map Control
- Rotate through positions strategically
- Maintain vision of key areas
- Cut off enemy escape routes
- Control supply stations (ammo/health)

#### Team Coordination
- Establish roles (scouts, heavies, support)
- Call out enemy positions continuously
- Coordinate pushes on objectives
- Maintain formation for maximum effectiveness

### Counter Strategies

**Against Light Tanks**: Use area damage; they can't absorb hits
**Against Heavy Tanks**: Flank them; their rotation is slow
**Against Tank Destroyers**: Close the distance; they're weak in close combat
**Against Artillery**: Move constantly; they need time to aim

## Troubleshooting

### Performance Issues

**Low FPS / Lag**
- Solution 1: Reduce graphics settings (Settings â†’ Graphics)
- Solution 2: Lower resolution or disable ray tracing
- Solution 3: Close background applications
- Solution 4: Update GPU drivers

**Game Crashes**
- Verify game files in Steam (Properties â†’ Local Files â†’ Verify)
- Update DirectX and graphics drivers
- Disable overclocking if applicable
- Check available disk space

### Connectivity Issues

**High Ping / Latency**
- Connect via ethernet instead of WiFi
- Choose servers closest to your location
- Restart your router
- Close bandwidth-heavy applications

**Connection Drops**
- Check your internet stability
- Update network drivers
- Disable VPN if using one
- Contact ISP if persistent

### Control Issues

**Keys Not Responding**
- Verify key bindings in Settings
- Restart the game
- Update keyboard drivers
- Try different keys to identify issue

**Controller Not Detected**
- Check controller USB connection or batteries
- Update controller drivers
- Rebind controls in Settings
- Try controller in another game to verify

### Account Issues

**Can't Log In**
- Verify username/password
- Reset password if forgotten
- Check account bans/restrictions
- Contact support if persistent

**Missing Progress**
- Check if logged into correct account
- Verify account linked to correct platform
- Clear cache and cookies
- Contact support with account details

### Game-Specific Issues

**Can't Connect to Server**
- Check internet connection
- Verify server status (check official website)
- Restart game and router
- Use alternate server location

**Matchmaking Takes Too Long**
- Expand search parameters in settings
- Play during peak hours
- Join community Discord for group queues

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the best tank for beginners?**
A: Medium tanks offer balanced stats. Try them first, then specialize.

**Q: How do I earn premium currency?**
A: Through battle passes, achievement rewards, and limited promotions. Never pay-to-win.

**Q: Can I play solo or is it multiplayer only?**
A: Both! Campaign is single-player, multiplayer is optional.

**Q: How often are updates released?**
A: Monthly balance patches, quarterly new content updates.

**Q: Is there a skill-based matchmaking system?**
A: Yes! Your rating is visible in your profile. Matches are balanced accordingly.

**Q: How do I report cheaters?**
A: Use the in-game report button or contact support with replay evidence.

**Q: Are cosmetics pay-to-win?**
A: No. All cosmetics are purely visual. No stat bonuses.

**Q: How can I join a clan?**
A: Visit the Clans section in the main menu or search for communities online.
